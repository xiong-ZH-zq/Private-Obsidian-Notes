TARGET DECK: Words::crim-

START
Word Power
incriminate
音标: 
释义:
To show evidence of involvement in a crime or fault.
发音:
例句:
- The muddy tracks leading to and from the cookie jar were enough to incriminate them.
例句翻译:
通向曲奇罐的泥印已经足够认定他们有罪了。
拓展:
意为“归罪于”，通常表示利用证据给罪犯定罪。
